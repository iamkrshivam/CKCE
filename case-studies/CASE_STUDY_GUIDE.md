# Case Study Contribution Guide

1. **Public sources only** – Use data from breach disclosure reports, academic papers, or authorised CTF events.
2. **No real IP addresses, hostnames, or credentials** – Replace with placeholders.
3. **Focus on defence** – Emphasise what went wrong from a controls perspective and how to prevent recurrence.
4. **Cross‑reference** – Link to the relevant domain’s `/case-studies/` folder.
5. **Review** – Maintainers may redact any potentially sensitive information.
