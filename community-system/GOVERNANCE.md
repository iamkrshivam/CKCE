# Governance Model

CKCE is a **Benevolent Dictator + Maintainer Team** model. The initial maintainers (see `MAINTAINERS.md`) have final say on structure and ethical compliance. Over time, decisions become more community‑driven.

## Decision‑making
- **Minor changes** (typos, clarifications) – Any maintainer can approve.
- **Major changes** (new domains, career system overhaul) – Requires consensus among maintainers and a 2‑week comment period.
- **Ethical disputes** – Escalated to the lead maintainer.

## Conflict resolution
If a contributor disagrees with a maintainer’s decision, they may open a GitHub issue labelled `governance`. A neutral maintainer will mediate. Ultimately, the lead maintainer’s decision is final.

## Amendments
Changes to this `GOVERNANCE.md` require a 75% vote of all maintainers and a public discussion issue for 30 days.
