# Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) Domain

What is it? – Aligning security with business goals and legal requirements.  
Why important? – Ensures accountability, reduces liability.  
Industry usage – Compliance audits (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI‑DSS), risk registers.  
Contributions – Control frameworks (NIST CSF, ISO 27001), risk assessment templates.  
Difficulty – Beginner (compliance basics), Intermediate (risk calculation), Advanced (third‑party risk), Research (automated compliance).  
Connections – Information Security, Data Security, IAM.  
Ethical – No actual internal policies from real organisations. Only generic templates.
