# Identity and Access Management (IAM) Domain

What is it? – Digital identities, authentication, authorisation, and auditing.  
Why important? – Weak IAM leads to account takeover and privilege escalation.  
Industry usage – SSO, MFA, RBAC, lifecycle management.  
Contributions – IAM architectures, protocol descriptions (OAuth2, SAML), Zero Trust principles.  
Difficulty – Beginner (passwords, MFA), Intermediate (OAuth flows), Advanced (federation, privileged access management), Research (decentralised identity).  
Connections – Cloud Security, Application Security, GRC.  
Ethical – No actual password cracking or bypass techniques.
