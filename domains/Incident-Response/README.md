# Incident Response Domain

What is it? – Structured approach to handling security incidents.  
Why important? – Minimises damage and recovery time.  
Industry usage – IR plans, containment strategies, forensic acquisition.  
Contributions – IR frameworks (NIST, SANS), roles, communication templates.  
Difficulty – Beginner (IR phases), Intermediate (containment types), Advanced (root cause analysis), Research (automated IR).  
Connections – Digital Forensics, SOC, GRC (legal reporting).  
Ethical – No live exploitation. Do not include actual ransom notes.
