# Threat Intelligence Domain

What is it? – Evidence‑based knowledge about threats and adversaries.  
Why important? – Proactive defence and informed decision making.  
Industry usage – IoC feeds, TTP analysis, threat modelling.  
Contributions – Intelligence lifecycle, data sources, analysis frameworks (MITRE ATT&CK).  
Difficulty – Beginner (IoCs vs TTPs), Intermediate (threat modelling), Advanced (adversary profiling), Research (automated intelligence).  
Connections – SOC, Incident Response, Vulnerability Management.  
Ethical – No real IoCs from active campaigns. Only academic or de‑conflicted examples.
