# Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) – Scope

## Core topics
- Security governance (policies, standards, procedures)
- Risk management (identification, analysis, evaluation, treatment)
- Compliance frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST CSF, SOC2, PCI‑DSS, HIPAA, GDPR)
- Audit management and evidence collection
- Third‑party risk management (TPRM)
- Business continuity and disaster recovery (BCP/DRP)
- Security awareness and training programmes
- Key risk indicators (KRIs) and key performance indicators (KPIs)

## Relationship with other domains
- **Information Security** – Technical controls under governance.
- **Data Security** – Compliance with data protection laws.
- **IAM** – Access governance and certification.
- **Vulnerability Management** – Risk‑based prioritisation.

## Learning structure
- **Beginner** – Governance vs management, basic risk terms, common frameworks.
- **Intermediate** – Risk assessment workshops, control mapping.
- **Advanced** – Audit evidence collection, compliance automation.
- **Research** – Integrated risk management (IRM), continuous compliance.

No actual internal policies or sensitive audit data. Only generic templates and public framework references.
