# Threat Intelligence – Scope

## Core topics
- Intelligence lifecycle (direction, collection, processing, analysis, dissemination)
- Types of threat intelligence (strategic, operational, tactical, technical)
- Data sources (open source, commercial, information sharing groups)
- Indicators of Compromise (IoCs) – IPs, domains, hashes, patterns
- Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs) – MITRE ATT&CK framework
- Threat actor profiling (motivation, capability, infrastructure)
- Intelligence sharing standards (STIX/TAXII, MISP)
- Threat hunting using intelligence

## Relationship with other domains
- **SOC** – Enriching alerts with IoCs.
- **Incident Response** – Identifying adversary TTPs.
- **Network Security** – Blocking known malicious IPs/domains.
- **Vulnerability Management** – Exploit intelligence.

## Learning structure
- **Beginner** – IoC types, open‑source feeds, MITRE ATT&CK basics.
- **Intermediate** – Threat modelling with ATT&CK, intelligence platforms.
- **Advanced** – Adversary simulation, diamond model, kill chain.
- **Research** – Automated intelligence, deception‑based intelligence.

No real, active IoCs or live threat feeds. Only placeholder examples and methodology descriptions.
