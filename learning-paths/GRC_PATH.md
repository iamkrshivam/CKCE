# Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) Learning Path

**Target career**: GRC Analyst, Compliance Officer  
**Prerequisites**: None – open to non‑technical backgrounds.  
**Duration**: 4–6 months.

## Phase 1: Fundamentals of GRC
- **Domains**: GRC (Beginner), Information Security (Beginner)
- **Skills**: Governance vs management, risk concepts, compliance drivers.
- **Prerequisites**: None.

## Phase 2: Risk Management Frameworks
- **Domains**: GRC (Intermediate), Information Security (Intermediate)
- **Skills**: ISO 31000, NIST RMF, risk assessment methodologies.
- **Prerequisites**: Phase 1.

## Phase 3: Compliance and Auditing
- **Domains**: GRC (Intermediate), Data Security (Beginner)
- **Skills**: Common frameworks (ISO 27001, SOC2, PCI‑DSS, HIPAA), audit evidence collection.
- **Prerequisites**: Phase 2.

## Phase 4: Third‑Party Risk and Business Continuity
- **Domains**: GRC (Advanced), Incident Response (Beginner)
- **Skills**: Vendor risk assessments, BCP/DRP, legal/regulatory reporting.
- **Prerequisites**: Phase 3.

## Capstone Project (skeleton)
- **Project**: Create a risk register and control map for a fictional organisation.
- **Domains**: GRC, Information Security.
- **Outcome**: Excel/Markdown table with risks, controls, and compliance mapping.
